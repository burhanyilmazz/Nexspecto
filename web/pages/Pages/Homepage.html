what is nexspecto?

We created this platform so that users can save with their savings.

The Nexspecto is a protocol on the Ethereum blockchain that determines money markets that are pools of assets algorithmically gotten together, based on the saving demand for the asset. Users of an asset interact immediately with the protocol, saving, installments, and qualifying to take target savings. Each money market is unique to an Ethereum asset and contains a transparent and publicly-inspectable ledger, with a record of all transactions.

The Nexspecto allows users to choose the amount of their periodic payment which depends on their target savings and opened pool liquidity size. Users must pay one percent of their 4 target savings as an organization fee which is non-refundable.

what’s saving assets?

  • RISK & LIQUIDATION

    If the users leave before the draw, the payments which they paid to the system will be refunded at the end of all payments have done except for the organization fee. On the other hand, after the draw, and given collateral, the remaining installments may be paid in exchange for the user’s nToken collateral with a non-payment penalty that eliminates the protocol’s risk.

  • COLLATERAL VALUE

    The collateral is had to be given when the user’s target savings is given by the system. Taken collateral is calculated as (Saving Target – Total Installments Paid) * 125/100. Only ETH or BTC is taken as collateral which protects the protocol from default risk.

  • GROUP

    Collaboration of customers with similar financing needs and installments.

  • GROUP OCCUPANCY RATE

    Groups that are not filled by 60% are kept on hold for the next month. Groups with a ratio above go to and start the financing revision according to the number of existing people.

  • DRAW

    The draw allows users to earn their targeted savings early, and they will be eagerly awaiting the results each month. After creating a group, only one user is paid the amount he plans to save every month by drawing lots. The draw will be held by Oracle services.

  • DELIVERY

    Financing fee given to the person who won the lottery. The Group receives the Financing fee. As soon as he receives it, he deposits BTC or ETH as collateral. Also send the token gift at the time of delivery.

  • DELAYING THE INSTALLMENT

    Nexspecto allows the user to delay a paying installment for one period of installment. The user who has payment difficulties will not be out of the system immediately with this unique property. It gives one more chance to the user to be in the system.

BREAKDOWN OF
OUR TOKEN RECIPIENTS

The Nexspecto protocol implements a tokenization strategy for liquidity providers. Upon savings, the user receives a corresponding number of derivative tokens. 70% of system earnings will be distributed to token holders and stake holders.

Pecto Token has a total fixed supply and is limited to 1,000,000,000 in total. The token has the characteristic of deflation. The number of tokens cannot be changed or lost, and token holders have full rights over the assets they hold.
